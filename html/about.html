RCC ACM Information

About Us

Hi, welcome to ACM, or the Association of Computing Machinery. We are a club dedicated to the knowledge and practice of computer science and computer programming. Here you can take part in any of our weekly lectures including coding in Unity, game design in Unreal, and our general ACM lectures that go over concepts important to competitive programming. Whether if you are an expert programmer or you are new to programming in general, new members are always welcomed.

How To Join

  • Join us here on discord for general announcements, club informaton, weekly meetings times, and to collaborate with other computer science students in a casual environment
  • Make a profile on this site to join the members page and access our online computer science practice judge
  • Talk to your professor if you are in a computer science class with Dr. Lehr or Professor Conrad.
  • Come to the ACM room in MLK 205 in person and ask for the president James or vice president Sebastian. Once there you can ask questions, check out a meeting, and join the official RCC ACM roster or you can just see how we do things

Contact Us

You can get ahold of us here

  • Message us on the official RCC ACM discord server
  • Email us at join@rccacm.com
  • Contact our advisor Dr. Mark Lehr or Professor Paul Conrad
  • Speak to us face to face in room MLK 205 before or after the meeting times which are listed on each groups page and in the schedule section of discord
